About

Flavors Management is a system created to help manage and organize flavor products efficiently. It allows users to add, update, and track inventory while ensuring accuracy and convenience in the process.

Purpose

The purpose of this system is to simplify product management for small businesses by providing an easy-to-use platform for monitoring and organizing product information.

Goal

Our goal is to help businesses save time, reduce manual errors, and maintain smooth operations through an efficient and user-friendly management system.
